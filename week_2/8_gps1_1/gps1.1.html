Top 3 reasons why pair-pramming is awesome and important

  1. 1+1=3 It optimizes your website and the quality of your code!
  2. Knowledge sharing You can learn from others and helps you know your code!
  3. More fun! Makes you more social! Noone wants to sit alone in his or her basement and do code

Top 3 reasons why pair programming is difficult or scary

  1. Makes you think out loud and that's difficult to do. You are not as much allowed to think in private
  2. It feels strange to "tell someone what to code" when navigating
  3. Difficult to strike a balance between sticking to your role and coding efficiently
